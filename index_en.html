Frontend Mentor

October 21, 2024

In my constant quest to improve my web development skills, I've recently embarked on a new adventure: the learning path at Frontend Mentor. This platform offers practical challenges that will allow me to expand my knowledge in HTML and CSS, as well as introduce me to the world of JavaScript, a language that has been unknown to me until now.

My goal is to complement this learning with my current projects. I believe that keeping myself busy will not only help me improve my skills but also maintain high motivation.

My First Achievement

With great satisfaction, I can say that I've already completed my first challenge on Frontend Mentor. Although it was a simple challenge, it gave me a sense of accomplishment and motivated me to continue on this learning path. Every small step counts, and I'm eager to face more complex challenges in the future. Frontend Mentor is an excellent option for practicing and improving frontend skills, and it has many great free challenges to keep you busy and motivated for a good while. So far, my experience has been satisfactory, and I highly recommend their challenges for web developers with different skill levels, as the site has challenges for all levels of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and a wide variety of useful frameworks in the frontend area.

You can follow my progress on my Frontend Mentor profile. Additionally, I'll be uploading my solutions to my GitHub repository, where you can see the code and my evolution in this Learning Path.
